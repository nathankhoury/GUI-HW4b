Dynamic Multiplication Table Generator

Welcome to the Dynamic Multiplication Table Generator! You can provide specific lower and upper bounds for both the columns and the rows of the multiplication table you wish to generate.

Below the fields you can choose to save a table in a new tab, or delete the currently selected tab. If you have saved multiple tables and wish to delete more than one at a time, you can simply check the boxes corresponding to the tabs you wish to delete in the "Saved Tables" section and remove them all at once by hitting the "Remove Table" button. If any boxes are selected when you hit the "Remove Table" button, the selected tabs will be removed NOT the currently selected tab (unless you have checked the box for it).
